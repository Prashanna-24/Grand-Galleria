Vincent van Gogh

(1853-1890)

Vincent van Gogh, the influential Dutch painter, created emotionally charged artworks, including "The Starry Night," despite struggling with mental health throughout his life.

Leonardo da Vinci

(1452-1519)

Leonardo da Vinci was a true Renaissance genius, renowned for his mastery in art, science, and invention. His enigmatic smile in the "Mona Lisa" captivates the world even today.

Pablo Picasso

(1881-1973)

Pablo Picasso revolutionized modern art with his innovative styles and techniques, leaving an indelible mark on the art world.

Raja Ravi Varma

(1848-1906)

Raja Ravi Varma, an influential Indian painter, blended European academic style with Indian themes, pioneering the popularization of Indian art and bridging cultural aesthetics.
